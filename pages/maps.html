🇺🇸 Hello! 👋
Welcome to the official web viewer of the radiation and sensor research group of the University of Alcalá. In it you will be able to view all the geospatial information used in the investigations on electromagnetic pollution carried out from the year 2017 to the present in the Madrid municipality of Meco. We leave you the link to the publications linked to this data: - We hope that all this information is useful to you. Any questions do not hesitate to contact us through the email enabled below.


Sobre el proyecto


Proyecto METEX-5G

El principal objetivo del proyecto es desarrollar una metodología de medida que permita caracterizar la variación de la exposición personal, temporal y espacial de la contaminación electromagnética.


🇺🇸 METEX-5G Project

The main objective of the project is to develop a measurement methodology that allows to characterize the variation of personal, temporal and spatial exposure to electromagnetic pollution.
